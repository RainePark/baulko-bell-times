About:

Baulkham Hills High School is a government-funded academically selective co-educational secondary day school, located in Baulkham Hills in the Hills District of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.

Consistently ranked as one of the state's top schools academically, the school was ranked second in the NSW Higher School Certificate in 2016, 2017 and 2020, ranked fifth in 2018, and ranked third in 2021. In 2010, the school was ranked the most popular school in NSW for high school applications.
